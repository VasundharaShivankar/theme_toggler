AI and the Future of Work in the Tech Industry

The emergence of artificial intelligence (AI) has been a pivotal point of innovation, reshaping the contours of industries worldwide. Today, the recent growth of AI has been unprecedented, fueled by advances in computational power, the availability of large datasets, and breakthroughs in machine learning algorithms, especially deep learning. This has led to transformative applications across industries, from autonomous vehicles and healthcare diagnostics to personalized recommendations and natural language processing tools.
